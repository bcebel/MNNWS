ABC Pulls Jimmy Kimmel From Air

ABC announced it was pulling Jimmy Kimmel’s late-night show 'indefinitely' following comments the host made about the man accused of fatally shooting conservative activist Charlie Kirk.

The decision followed pressure from F.C.C. Chairman Brendan Carr, who suggested the agency might take action against ABC. President Trump praised the move, calling it 'Great News for America.'

Major ABC affiliates, including Nexstar and Sinclair, had already announced they would preempt the show. Democrats denounced the move as an attack on free speech.
