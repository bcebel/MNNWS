RippleFest Texas: A Weekend of Heavy Rock in Austin

Austin music fans, get ready for RippleFest Texas, a weekend-long festival dedicated to heavy rock, stoner metal, and psychedelic grooves. The event takes over The Far Out Lounge & Stage from Thursday, September 18th, through Sunday, the 21st, making it the epicenter of the city's rock scene.

The Main Event: RippleFest Texas

RippleFest Texas is a multi-day festival showcasing a massive lineup of heavy rock titans. This is a can't-miss event for anyone who loves loud guitars and powerful riffs. The Austin music scene will be buzzing all weekend.

Who to See at this Heavy Rock Festival

The lineup for RippleFest Texas is packed with talent. Some of the must-see bands include:

  • Weedeater: Legendary sludge metal from North Carolina.
  • Whores: Intense and chaotic noise rock from Atlanta.
  • Mothership: Dallas-based classic rock and heavy metal.
  • ASG: High-energy stoner rock.
  • Mondo Generator: The project of former Kyuss and Queens of the Stone Age bassist Nick Oliveri.

With dozens of bands across multiple stages, RippleFest Texas is the place to be for your live music fix. If you're a fan of Austin music, you won't want to miss this heavy rock festival.
