Austin's Vibe All Weekend: Your Guide to RippleFest Texas!

Heads up, Austin music lovers! While the week's one-off shows have wrapped up, the main event is just getting started. RippleFest Texas is taking over The Far Out Lounge & Stage all weekend long, from Thursday, September 18th, through Sunday, the 21st. If you're looking for the epicenter of the city's rock scene, this is it.

The Main Event: A Weekend of Heavy Rock

RippleFest is a sonic explosion of heavy rock, stoner metal, and psychedelic grooves. This multi-day festival has curated a massive lineup of genre titans, making it a can't-miss event for anyone who loves loud guitars and powerful riffs.

Who to See

The lineup is stacked from top to bottom. Some of the heavy hitters you can catch throughout the weekend include:

  • Weedeater: The legendary sludge metal band from North Carolina is sure to deliver a crushing set.
  • Whores: This Atlanta-based noise rock trio is known for their intense and chaotic live shows.
  • Mothership: Hailing from Dallas, this band brings a supersonic brew of classic rock and heavy metal.
  • ASG: A long-running favorite in the stoner rock scene, ASG's sets are always a high-energy affair.
  • Mondo Generator: The brainchild of former Kyuss and Queens of the Stone Age bassist Nick Oliveri, this band is a force of nature.

And that's just a small sample. With dozens of bands playing across multiple stages, there's always something new to discover.

If you're looking for your live music fix this weekend, The Far Out Lounge & Stage is the place to be. Get out there and support this incredible festival!
