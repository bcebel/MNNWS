The Wait is Over: Coachella Unveils Stacked 2026 Lineup

Music fans, get ready to return to the desert! The official lineup for the 2026 Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival has just dropped, and it's a spectacular blend of global superstars, indie favorites, and exciting newcomers. This year's festival promises a diverse and unforgettable experience with headliners Sabrina Carpenter, Justin Bieber, and Karol G.

Friday: Sabrina Carpenter, The xx, and Electronic Beats

Kicking off the festival on Friday is pop sensation Sabrina Carpenter. She's joined by the iconic English indie rock band The xx and the infectious electronic beats of Disclosure. The first day also features a powerful and eclectic undercard, including the ethereal sounds of Ethel Cain, R&B artist Dijon, and the soulful Teddy Swims. Fans of classic new wave will be thrilled to see Devo on the lineup, while rap and pop are well-represented by Sexyy Red, Central Cee, and Foster the People.

Saturday: Justin Bieber, The Strokes, and a Supergroup Debut

Saturday is set to be a massive day with global superstar Justin Bieber taking the headlining spot. The day also features the legendary New York rock band The Strokes and the highly anticipated debut of Nine Inch Noize, a supergroup collaboration between industrial rock pioneers Nine Inch Nails and electronic producer Boys Noize. The lineup continues with R&B star GIVÄ’ON, social media personality turned pop artist Addison Rae, and the experimental sounds of Labrinth and David Byrne.

Sunday: Karol G, Young Thug, and Dance Music Legends

Closing out the festival on Sunday is Colombian reggaeton queen Karol G. The final day is packed with heavy hitters, including rapper Young Thug and K-Pop legends BIGBANG. The dance music vibes are strong with sets from mainstage progressive house artist Anyma, the legendary Kaskade, and powerhouse trio Major Lazer. Punk icon Iggy Pop will bring his raw energy, alongside the avant-garde pop of FKA twigs and the critically acclaimed indie rock of Wet Leg.

A Festival for Everyone

Once again, Coachella has delivered a lineup that spans genres and generations. From pop and rock to hip-hop, electronic, and global music, there's something for every music lover. Get ready for another incredible year in the desert. See you there!
