Coachella 2026 Lineup Revealed: Headliners and Highlights

The official lineup for the 2026 Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival has been announced, featuring headliners Sabrina Carpenter, Justin Bieber, and Karol G. The diverse lineup promises a memorable festival experience with a mix of global superstars and indie favorites.

Coachella 2026 Headliners

The Coachella 2026 headliners are Sabrina Carpenter, Justin Bieber, and Karol G. These three artists represent a diverse range of genres and are sure to draw huge crowds. The full lineup for Coachella 2026 is packed with talent across all three days, and these headliners are a big reason why.

Friday's Lineup

Sabrina Carpenter will headline on Friday, joined by The xx and Disclosure. The undercard includes Ethel Cain, Dijon, Teddy Swims, Devo, Sexyy Red, Central Cee, and Foster the People.

Saturday's Lineup

Justin Bieber is the headliner for Saturday, with The Strokes and the debut of supergroup Nine Inch Noize also on the bill. Other performers include GIVÄ’ON, Addison Rae, Labrinth, and David Byrne. This is a huge lineup for the second day of Coachella 2026.

Sunday's Lineup

Karol G will close out the festival on Sunday. The final day also features Young Thug, BIGBANG, Anyma, Kaskade, Major Lazer, Iggy Pop, FKA twigs, and Wet Leg. The Coachella 2026 lineup is one of the most exciting yet, with a strong finish from these headliners.
